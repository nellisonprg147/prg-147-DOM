Planing a Cycling Trip

navigation Route Planning

When planning your route, Google street view comes in handy. It will help in checking the road conditions and help you stay out of bad neighborhoods.

local_cafe Meal Planning

Meal planning is very important. Every meal, snack, and luxury item has to be taken into account for size, weight, and nutritional value.

directions_bike Required Gear Planning

Other than a bike, there is all kinds of gear that is necessary. Especially if you are camping at your overnight stops.

hot_tub Rest Stops Every 20 Miles

Planning rest stops along your journy is also important. Every 20 miles or so you will need to fill up on water, and its a great idea to give the legs a rest.

hotel Hotel / Camping Accommodations Planning

If you are not camping, a hotel is a great way to go. A lot less gear to carry and a much more comfortable bed.

beach_access Rain Contingency Plan

It always seems to rain. Always pack rain gear unless you plan on traveling in the desert.
